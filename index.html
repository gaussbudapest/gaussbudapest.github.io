Welcome Dear Sightseer,

If you would like to get to know about the famous sights of Budapest in an unusual way, this is the best program you could have ever found. This program is a kind of virtual, refresher-course tour which offers you the opportunity to ramble in the capital city of Hungary while sitting in front of your computer.

First, you can read a short guide—spiced with photos—about each scene. You can control your tour of the city on the map so as not to get lost. Then you can try a brain-teaser by attemping to solve a mathematical problem which is related to the given scene. Try to solve them alone first, please. Think about what a great sense of achievement it would be to realise that you know the ins and outs of maths. And, if it doesn’t turn out you shouldn’t fell sad. Just read through the solutions which can be found below each question. If you do that, you will be able to solve a similar question easily when it turns up again.

After you’ve roved over the whole city—from the Ferenc Liszt International Airport to the Western Railway Station—you will have the chance to complete a quiz to check your memory.

Finally, when you are extremely tired, you can visit our gallery of photos. Here just lean back, relax, and indulge in nostalgia.

Have a great time!
