Solution (Planetarium)

1. If anyone can sit anywhere, the 9 students can take a seat in 9! ways.

2. If we don't let the two girls sit next to each other, then they must be treated as one element so they can take seat in 8! ways.

3. The order of the girls is also important; it can be ab or ba.

4. That is why 2·8! is the number of ways when the two girls are sitting next to each other.

5. The number of proper solutions, except the cases when the girls are sitting next to each other:

9! − 2·8! = 362880-80640 = 282240

The total number of different arrangements is 282240.
